My recommendation
If I were designing Kontra today, it would look like this:
User	Authentication
Owner (payer)	✅ Email + OTP
Inspector	❌ Secure invite link
Attorney	❌ Secure invite link
Insurance	❌ Secure invite link
Borrower	❌ Secure invite link
Lender	❌ Secure invite link
Investor	❌ Secure invite link
Engineer	❌ Secure invite link
Servicer	❌ Secure invite link
I would not remove owner authentication. The owner has ongoing resources to manage—deal rooms, billing, payments, and link revocation—so an identity is valuable there.
For everyone else, I'd preserve the frictionless invite-link experience. In my view, that's one of Kontra's strongest product decisions because it minimizes barriers for occasional participants in a transaction while still giving the paying customer a secure, persistent workspace.
