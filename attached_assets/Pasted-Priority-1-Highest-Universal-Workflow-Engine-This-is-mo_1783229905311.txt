One suggestion I'd make to your developer
I'd ask them to avoid proving the architecture with another CRE template. That demonstrates flexibility within one domain, but it doesn't really validate that the platform is industry-agnostic.
A stronger proof would be:

Template 1: Commercial Real Estate Acquisition (your current strength)
Template 2: Business Acquisition (a fundamentally different workflow)
If those two can run on the exact same engine—with different stages, document types, participants, AI prompts, and dashboards—then you've demonstrated that Kontra is truly a platform rather than a CRE application with configurable screens.
That kind of proof will also make it much easier to expand into new markets over time while keeping CRE as your initial beachhead.
