One thing I'd add
Every checklist item should have
Required

or

Optional

And maybe even

Typically requested

For example:

Phase I Environmental

Required

Why?

Most institutional lenders require a current Phase I ESA before closing.
That helps less experienced borrowers.
My recommendation
I would not chase every lender's checklist.
Instead, I'd aim for:

A strong default checklist that covers the majority of standard commercial real estate transactions.
Transaction- and property-specific templates that add or remove items as appropriate.
The ability for the owner or lender to add custom checklist items when needed.
That approach is more scalable than trying to encode every lender's individual process.
