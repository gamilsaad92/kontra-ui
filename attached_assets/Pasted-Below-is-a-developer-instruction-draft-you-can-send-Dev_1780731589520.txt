Developer Instructions: Convert Current Kontra Into Public CRE Marketplace + Operating System

We need to reposition Kontra from a closed lender-only servicing platform into a public commercial real estate marketplace and asset operating system.

The goal is to make the public marketplace, property pages, service provider directory, AI tools, and logged-in workspace feel like one unified product — not two separate interfaces.

1. Core Product Direction

Kontra should become:

A public marketplace and operating system for commercial real estate assets, data, inspections, service providers, and investors.

The platform should support:

1. Public property discovery.
2. Public service provider marketplace.
3. Property intelligence pages.
4. AI document review tools.
5. Property workspaces after login.
6. Future investor/watchlist functionality.
7. Future tokenization module, but not the primary focus now.

Do not position the product as “loan servicing only.” It should be broader: CRE asset intelligence, compliance, inspections, and marketplace.

⸻

2. Current Problem to Fix

Right now, the issue is that the public website/marketplace and the old logged-in Kontra platform feel disconnected.

When users click Login or Sign Up, they are sent into the old platform experience, which feels separate from the new marketplace concept.

This needs to be fixed.

The public marketplace and logged-in app should use the same design system, navigation logic, and product structure.

⸻

20. Final Goal

When a user visits Kontra, the experience should feel like:

1. Discover CRE assets.
2. Analyze property risk.
3. Connect with service providers.
4. Upload documents.
5. Manage inspections.
6. Track compliance.
7. Save assets to watchlist.
8. Eventually invest or tokenize.

The platform should no longer feel like a private lender servicing portal only.
